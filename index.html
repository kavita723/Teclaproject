Electric vehicles use one or more electric motors for propulsion. These motors are powered by electricity stored in rechargeable batteries. When the vehicle is in motion, the electric motor(s) convert the electrical energy into mechanical energy.

The cost of charging an electric vehicle depends on local electricity rates and the vehicle's battery size. On average, it can range between ₹100 to ₹300 for a full charge in India.
